The Dark Knight is a 2008 superhero film directed by Christopher Nolan, based on the DC Comics character Batman. The film is a sequel to 2005's Batman Begins, and stars Christian Bale as Bruce Wayne / Batman, Heath Ledger as the Joker, and Aaron Eckhart as Harvey Dent / Two-Face. In the film, Batman sets out to stop the Joker from wreaking havoc on Gotham City, while struggling with his own inner demons.
